Welcome to Black Fightday, in this game you battle for who gets the most points. You get points by collecting various items and depositing them in your basket. The more expencive the product, the more points it's worth. There are also powerups that give various effects.

When a player picks up an item the other player can fight to steal the item or kill their opponent. you can also choose to throw an item, either towards an opponent to damage them, or to cash in your item from a distance.
